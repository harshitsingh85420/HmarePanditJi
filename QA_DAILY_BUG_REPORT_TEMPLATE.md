## Appendix: Quick Reference

### Bug Severity Definitions

**P0 - Critical 🔴**
- App crash, data loss, registration blocked
- SLA: Fix within 4 hours
- Testing: Blocked until fixed

**P1 - High 🟠**
- Major feature broken, workaround exists
- SLA: Fix within 24 hours
- Testing: Can continue with workaround

**P2 - Medium 🟡**
- Minor feature broken, does not block core functionality
- SLA: Fix within 3 days
- Testing: Can continue normally

**P3 - Low 🟢**
- Cosmetic issue, typo, nice-to-have
- SLA: Fix before launch (best effort)
- Testing: No impact

1. **Blocker > 4 hours:** Post in Slack `#hmarepanditji-dev`
2. **No response in 2 hours:** DM Senior Dev directly
